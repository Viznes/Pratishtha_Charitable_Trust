We're Pratishtha.

Unlocking the warmth of unconditional love, 'Pratishtha Charitable Trust' stands as a sanctuary of compassion for abandoned elderly parents. With hearts devoted to healing and care, we embrace those left behind in their golden years, nurturing them with dignity and respect. Through a nurturing home and unwavering support, we offer a second chance at happiness, enriching lives with nourishment, medical care, and the warmth of a loving community. Join us in illuminating the lives of these forgotten heroes, and together, let's create a world where no parent feels abandoned, and love knows no bounds.
